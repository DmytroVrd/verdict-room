## NOTES FOR RECORDING

- **Total length target:** 4:50–5:00. Hard ceiling — lablab cuts at 5 min.
- **Pace:** ~130 words per minute. Read aloud BEFORE recording, time yourself.
- **Pauses:** real silence (0.5s) between slides reads better than rushing.
- **The MOMENT:** slow down at Compliance recruitment in demo — 2x slower than rest.
- **Words to pronounce slowly:** GDPR (G-D-P-R), API (A-P-I), B2B (B-to-B), GPT-4.1 (G-P-T four point one).
- **If you flub a word:** keep going for 5 sec, then say "let me retake that line" — easier to edit.
- **Mic:** USB headset is fine. Quiet room, no echo. Phone in airplane mode.
